Healthcare Infrastructure in Eastern Cape Province (South Africa)

hospitals, clinics, power supply and healthcare districts

This Open Data map was commissioned by Konektaz (now Healthsites.io) and was made using Open Data. Feature data also includes power lines and was downloaded from the website of the Eastern Cape Provincial Spatial Development Plan. Boundaries were made available on the Municipal Demarcation Board website. The map was created using TileMill and styled with CartoCSS. Hovering over a health district brings up its name in the top right hand corner, clinic names appear as you zoom in.
